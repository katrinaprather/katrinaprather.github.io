Who Am I - Take A Peek.....

I am a Front End Web Developer and lover of all things technology! Being engaged in work that allows me to be detail-oriented, reasonably challenged, solve problems, create efficiency and be a little creative brings a smile to my face.


I have a varied IT background. My undergraduate degree is in Computer Information Systems and my graduate degree is in Business Education. I started out in undergrad as an intern working with the University's Webmaster. Then moved to desktop support at at technical college. Next, I taught Computer Science and Business Education classes to high school students. My journey then lead me to IT Healthcare as a Benefit Coder where I use a proprietary language to code how benefits pay when insurance claims process through our system.


I believe in being a life long learner. Therefore, I aspire to become a master of my craft. In the near future, I plan to re-learn Java and learn either the MEAN or LAMP stack.


Outside of learning, I enjoy relaxing at coffee shops, book stores, parks, being a foodie, basketball, real estate and hanging out with my French Bulldog Brooklyn.
